\section*{Motivation}
The algebraic strict model \(m_n = m_0 n^2/\alpha(m_n)\) fails by orders of magnitude.
We replace it by a non-perturbative, topology-driven relation:
\begin{equation}
  m_n \;=\; \mu_\star \, Z(\kappa,p)\,
  \exp\!\Bigg(-\frac{S_0(p,\kappa)+n\,S_1(p,\kappa)+n^2\,S_2(p,\kappa)}{\alpha(\mu=m_n)}\Bigg),
\end{equation}
with \(n\in\{1,3,9\}\) for \((e,\mu,\tau)\).
Here \(S_i\) are dimensionless action functionals derived from toroidal geometry and
Hecke sector \(p\), while \(Z\) is a determinant prefactor. No empirical fits are used.
