\subsection{Connection as a Derived Quantity}

As with all classical geometric objects in UBT, the connection is a real projection
of a fundamental biquaternionic object:
\[
  \Gamma^\lambda_{\mu\nu} = \mathrm{Re}\!\left(\Omega^\lambda_{\mu\nu}\right),
\]
where $\Omega^\lambda_{\mu\nu} \in \mathbb{C}\otimes\mathbb{H}$ is the biquaternionic
connection derived from the biquaternionic metric $\mathcal{G}_{\mu\nu}$
(see \texttt{canonical/geometry/metric.tex}).

The Levi-Civita connection is uniquely fixed by:
\begin{enumerate}
  \item Metric compatibility: $\nabla_\rho g_{\mu\nu} = 0$
  \item Torsion-free: $\Gamma^\lambda_{\mu\nu} = \Gamma^\lambda_{\nu\mu}$
\end{enumerate}
yielding the standard formula
\[
  \Gamma^\lambda_{\mu\nu} = \tfrac{1}{2}\,g^{\lambda\rho}
  \!\left(\partial_\mu g_{\nu\rho} + \partial_\nu g_{\mu\rho} - \partial_\rho g_{\mu\nu}\right).
\]
