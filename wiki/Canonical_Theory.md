## Scope Policy

See [`canonical/SCOPE.md`](https://github.com/DavJ/unified-biquaternion-theory/blob/master/canonical/SCOPE.md)
for what is and is not included in canonical UBT.

Excluded from canonical:
- Conceptual-only or unformalized material
- Speculative extensions (consciousness, cosmological extras)
- Incomplete derivations without explicit gap inventory

These belong in [Research Tracks](Research_Tracks) or [Speculative Extensions](Speculative_Extensions).
