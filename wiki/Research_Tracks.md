## Promotion to Canonical

Research track material may be promoted to `canonical/` once it satisfies:

1. Internal consistency with canonical UBT
2. Mathematical closure (no unresolved proof gaps)
3. Compatibility with all canonical invariant tests
4. Review by author

The promotion workflow is documented in `AGENTS.md`.
