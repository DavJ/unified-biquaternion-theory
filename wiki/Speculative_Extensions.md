# Speculative Extensions

Speculative extensions are **conceptual extrapolations** beyond the canonical theory.
They are not part of canonical UBT and are maintained separately to preserve the
rigour of the core theory.

**Directory**: [`speculative_extensions/`](https://github.com/DavJ/unified-biquaternion-theory/tree/main/speculative_extensions)

---

> ⚠️ **Important**: Material in this section is speculative. It has not been
> mathematically proved and may be inconsistent with canonical UBT.
> Do not cite these pages as established results.

## Complex Consciousness Theory (CCT)

The Complex Consciousness Theory applies UBT principles to model consciousness
as a physical phenomenon in complex-time phase space.

**Key claims** (all speculative):
- Consciousness corresponds to excitations ("psychons") in the imaginary time sector
- The brain acts as a Theta Resonator coupling to ψ-modes
- Complex time τ = t + iψ provides a substrate for subjective experience

**Status**: **Speculative** — no mathematical derivation or experimental validation

**Directory**: [`speculative_extensions/consciousness/`](https://github.com/DavJ/unified-biquaternion-theory/tree/main/speculative_extensions/consciousness)

## Closed Timelike Curves (CTCs)

UBT's complex time metric admits solutions with closed loops in the τ plane.
These are studied as speculative CTC (time travel) solutions.

**Status**: **Speculative** — no experimental prediction or observational test
has been formulated.

---

## Separation Policy

Speculative material is kept in `speculative_extensions/` to:

1. Prevent contamination of the canonical theory with unproven claims
2. Allow free exploration without risking theory regression
3. Maintain clear labelling for readers and reviewers

Any material from speculative extensions that achieves mathematical closure
and consistency with canonical UBT may be reviewed for promotion to
[Research Tracks](Research_Tracks) or [Canonical Theory](Canonical_Theory).
