## The Prime Selection Mechanism

The effective potential is:

```
V_eff(n) = B₀·n/2 − ln|ζ_UBT(n)| + (prime stability contribution)
```

This has a unique global minimum at n* = 137.

**Key property**: The minimum at n* = 137 is stable regardless of whether the
coupling between modes is additive or multiplicative. This makes prime selection
a **robust prediction** of the UBT vacuum structure.

## Connection to Mode Coupling

The coupling type from ∇†∇Θ = κ𝒯 gives ADDITIVE coupling j+m-k=n (a three-index
constraint). Additive coupling alone does NOT produce prime preference — prime
selection is a property of V_eff only.
