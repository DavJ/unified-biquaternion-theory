# Mirror Sector

The mirror sector is a **motivated conjecture** arising from the twin prime structure
(137, 139) in the Hecke analysis. It predicts a parallel habitable sector with
fine structure constant α' = 1/139.

**Canonical source**: [`ARCHIVE/archive_legacy/consolidation_project/mirror_sector/`](https://github.com/DavJ/unified-biquaternion-theory/tree/main/ARCHIVE/archive_legacy/consolidation_project/mirror_sector)

## Summary

| Sector | Prime | α⁻¹ | Hecke Set |
|--------|-------|------|-----------|
| Our sector | 137 | 137 | Set A |
| Mirror sector | 139 | 139 | Set B |

The two primes are **twin primes** (differ by 2). Mirror matter couples to our sector
only via gravity and is a viable dark matter candidate.
