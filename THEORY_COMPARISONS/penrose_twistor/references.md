# References: Penrose Twistor Theory

## Primary Sources

### Books

1. **Penrose, R., & Rindler, W. (1984)**  
   *Spinors and Space-Time, Volume 1: Two-Spinor Calculus and Relativistic Fields*  
   Cambridge University Press  
   ISBN: 978-0521337076  
   - Canonical reference for spinor formalism and twistor basics

2. **Penrose, R., & Rindler, W. (1986)**  
   *Spinors and Space-Time, Volume 2: Spinor and Twistor Methods in Space-Time Geometry*  
   Cambridge University Press  
   ISBN: 978-0521347860  
   - Advanced twistor theory, curved spacetime applications

3. **Penrose, R. (2004)**  
   *The Road to Reality: A Complete Guide to the Laws of the Universe*  
   Jonathan Cape  
   ISBN: 978-0679454434  
   - Chapter 33: Twistors and curved spacetime
   - Accessible introduction for general audience

4. **Huggett, S. A., & Tod, K. P. (1994)**  
   *An Introduction to Twistor Theory* (Second Edition)  
   Cambridge University Press  
   ISBN: 978-0521456890  
   - Comprehensive textbook on twistor methods
