**Target**: Journal of Negative Results or dedicated section in PRD

1. Planck CMB TT comb null result (p = 0.919)

**Challenges**:

- May be seen as "failed prediction" rather than "successful science"

**Recommendation**: Bundle with methodology paper on forensic testing protocols.

## Writing Guidelines

### Language to Use

✅ **For General Framework**:
- "UBT generalizes General Relativity"
- "In the real limit, UBT reproduces Einstein's equations"
- "Derives SM gauge group from geometric structure"
- "Predicts particle masses from topology"

✅ **For Predictions**:
- "UBT predicts α⁻¹ = 137.036"
- "Confirmed to 0.00003% precision"
- "No free parameters fitted"
- "Multiple independent derivations converge"

✅ **For Null Results**:
- "Planck CMB TT comb test yielded null result (p = 0.919)"
- "This falsifies the macroscopic TT comb channel"
- "Theory refinement needed for this prediction"
- "Null results guide future research direction"

✅ **For Hubble Latency**:
- "Can be interpreted as synchronization bias"
- "Consistent with observed Hubble tension magnitude"
- "Testable via standard sirens"
- "Distinct from early dark energy models"

### Language to Avoid

❌ **Overstated Claims**:
- "Proves UBT is correct" → Use "Consistent with UBT predictions"
- "Solves the Hubble tension" → Use "Provides interpretation of Hubble tension"
- "Only correct theory" → Use "Makes testable predictions"
- "Final theory of everything" → Use "Unification framework"

❌ **Speculative Content**:
- "Consciousness as physical phenomenon" → Core UBT makes NO consciousness claims
- "Time travel via CTCs" → NOT a core UBT prediction
- "Multiverse interpretation" → NOT required by UBT
- "Computational universe" → Architectural framework, not proven

❌ **Anti-GR Language**:
- "Alternative to General Relativity" → Use "Generalization of GR"
- "Replacement for Einstein's theory" → Use "Embeds GR as real limit"
- "GR is wrong" → Use "GR is exact in real limit"

## Reviewer Concerns to Address

### Expected Objections

1. **"Biquaternions are unfamiliar formalism"**
   - Response: Provide clear mathematical definitions
   - Include worked examples
   - Show equivalence to tensors in familiar limits

2. **"Discrete-time architecture is speculative"**
   - Response: Separate predictions that don't depend on discreteness
   - Emphasize geometric predictions (α, m_e) work in continuum limit
   - Hubble latency is most dependent on discrete architecture

3. **"Why should we believe complex time exists?"**
   - Response: Imaginary component ψ is invisible to classical observations
   - Predictions arise from mathematical structure, not from observing ψ
   - Standard QFT uses imaginary time in Wick rotation

4. **"CMB null result suggests theory is wrong"**
   - Response: Null result falsifies ONE prediction channel (TT comb)
   - Does NOT falsify theory as whole
   - Redirects research to phase-sensitive channels
   - Shows theory IS falsifiable (good for science)

5. **"Fine structure prediction sounds too good to be true"**
   - Response: Provide complete derivation with NO fitted parameters
   - Show multiple independent approaches converge
   - Include guard tests in supplementary material
   - Demonstrate NO empirical tuning
