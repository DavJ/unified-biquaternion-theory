# Unified Biquaternion Theory (UBT) - Overview

## What is UBT?

Unified Biquaternion Theory (UBT) is a theoretical physics framework that attempts to unify General Relativity, Quantum Field Theory, and Standard Model symmetries within a single biquaternionic field Θ(q,τ) defined over complex time τ = t + iψ.

**Core idea**: Spacetime and gauge fields are different aspects of a unified geometric structure described by biquaternions (quaternions with complex coefficients).

## What UBT Is

### A Unification Framework

UBT provides a mathematical structure that:
- **Embeds General Relativity** (exact agreement in real limit)
- **Derives Standard Model gauge group** SU(3)×SU(2)×U(1) from geometry
- **Predicts particle masses** from topological/geometric structure
- **Unifies gravity and quantum fields** in single Θ field

### A Testable Theory

UBT makes **precise quantitative predictions**:
- **α⁻¹ = 137.036** (confirmed to 0.00003%)
- **m_e ≈ 0.510 MeV** (confirmed to ~0.2%)
- **No CMB TT comb** at periods Δℓ ∈ {8,16,32,64,128,255} (confirmed null)
- **Hubble latency**: δ ≈ 7.8% clock shear (testable with standard sirens)

### A Work in Progress

UBT is NOT a final theory:
- Some predictions confirmed
- Some predictions null
- Many predictions untested
- Subject to refinement based on empirical results

## What UBT Is NOT

### Not a Replacement for General Relativity

UBT **generalizes** GR, does not replace it:
- In real limit (ψ → 0), UBT = exact GR
- All GR confirmations validate UBT's real sector
- Imaginary components invisible to classical observations

**Language**: "UBT generalizes GR", NOT "alternative to GR"

### Not About Consciousness

**UBT core theory makes NO claims about consciousness.**
- Consciousness content is in `SPECULATIVE/`
- "Psychons" are speculative extensions, NOT core UBT
- Kept separate for scientific integrity

### Not Proven

UBT has:
- ✅ Confirmed predictions (α, m_e)
- ❌ Null results (Planck CMB)
- ⏳ Untested predictions (many)

**Status**: Promising but unproven theoretical framework

### Not Pseudoscience

UBT follows scientific method:
- **Testable predictions** (precise, quantitative)
- **Court-grade testing** (SHA-256 manifests, pre-registration)
- **Null results documented** (not hidden)
- **Speculation clearly separated** (see `SPECULATIVE/`)

## Key Achievements

### Confirmed Predictions

1. **Fine Structure Constant**
   - Prediction: α⁻¹ = 137.036
   - Experiment: α⁻¹ = 137.035999084 ± 0.000000021
   - **Error: 0.00003%** (4 decimal places agreement)
   - **No parameters fitted** - pure geometric calculation

2. **Electron Mass**
   - Prediction: m_e ≈ 0.510 MeV (baseline + QED)
   - Experiment: m_e = 0.51099895 ± 0.00000015 MeV
   - **Error: ~0.2%**
   - **No experimental input** - topological soliton calculation
