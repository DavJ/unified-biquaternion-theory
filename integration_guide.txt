=== UBT α(μ) Appendix Integration Guide ===

1) Put the appendix file next to your main LaTeX sources:
   - appendix_C_geometry_alpha_v2.tex

2) In your main .tex (usually main.tex), add near the end (before \end{document}):
   \clearpage
   \input{appendix_C_geometry_alpha_v2.tex}

3) Make sure your preamble loads standard math packages:
   \usepackage{amsmath, amssymb}
   \usepackage{hyperref}
   \usepackage{geometry} % optional
   \usepackage{graphicx} % if needed

4) If you reference in-text:
   - Heck worlds explanation: see Appendix C.2
   - Stationary FP: Eq. (C.\ref{eq:fp}) and (C.\ref{eq:Tpsi})
   - Curvature → beta-flow: Eqs. (C.\ref{eq:gaussian})–(C.\ref{eq:alpha-two-loop})
   - Prime-selection baseline: Sec. C.5

5) Build:
   pdflatex main_with_appendix_example.tex
   bibtex (if you have refs)
   pdflatex main_with_appendix_example.tex
   pdflatex main_with_appendix_example.tex
