## Research Notes

**Mathematical Level**: Extensions e05-e07 require graduate-level knowledge of:
- Differential geometry (fiber bundles, line bundles)
- Lie groups and algebras (SU(2,2), Lie exponential)
- Complex geometry (Kähler manifolds, modular forms)
- Spinor calculus (Clifford algebras, spinor representations)

**Implementation**: All extensions use:
- SymPy for symbolic computation
- Explicit matrix constructions (no black-box libraries)
- Numerical validation where symbolic computation is intractable

**Limitations**: Current implementations are:
- Exploratory (not optimized for production)
- Limited to flat spacetime (no curvature)
- Numerical precision issues in conformal transformations (acceptable for proofs-of-concept)
